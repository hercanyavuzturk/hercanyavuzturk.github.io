PURPOSE OF SAHADA

Application for football lovers!

Want to go to the football field but your player is missing? Looking for an opponent team but can't find it? Let's solve this problem together!

The SAHADA brings football lovers together on a single platform. You can find players for your team or join another team by creating your own profiles with its user-friendly interface. You can also find the most suitable player for your team by looking at the details on the player cards.
